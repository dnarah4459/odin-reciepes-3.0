Fries

Fries P is a gourmet fast-casual restaurant that reimagines the classic french fry experience. Specializing in a diverse range of fries, from crispy shoestring to hearty steak-cut, Fries P offers an array of unique toppings and inventive dipping sauces. Each serving is crafted with high-quality, locally-sourced potatoes and cooked to perfection, ensuring a delightful crunch and flavor in every bite. Whether you're in the mood for traditional salted fries or adventurous combinations like truffle oil and parmesan, Fries P provides a fresh and exciting take on this beloved comfort food, making it the go-to destination for fry enthusiasts.

Ingredients

      Potato
      Salt

Steps

      put in deep oil
      fry till crisp
